About wondererwanderer

We make travel planning easier

We understand the pains of travel planning, the amount of time spend on research, the difficulties in organising all the travel details and getting everyone on the same page. It is the reason why many people fear travel planning.

wondererwanderer is a platform that simplify the planning process, making it easy to create activities in your itinerary and automatically organises them for you.

We believe the good things in life should be shared, sometimes selectively. We make it easy for you to share your itinerary with your friends. We have heard the phrase "share with me your itinerary" so many times and we make it happen.

There are so many things that we are going to implement to make the process even easier. This is just the beginning. We are excited and you should be too.
